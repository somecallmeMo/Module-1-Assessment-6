Our Story

Wojtek - pronounced: voy-tehk. Founded in 2024, Wojtek Armaments started as simple plan to source quality military grade equipment and apparrel without the hefty price tag.

At Wojtek Armaments we source all products solely from Turkey. Thanks to our founder, Muhammad Haffejee, attaining suppliers in Turkey who employ and support locals that were/are affetced by the tragic earthquakes that have shaken many major and minor cities.

We pride ourselves in providing South African defense industry personel with quality, affordable, robust equipment and tactical gear that is guranteed to survive the toughest rocks, hard places and anything in-between.
